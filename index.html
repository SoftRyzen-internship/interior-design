About us

WE BELIEVE IN QUALITY

Interio design studio has been on the market for over 5 years and it is a dynamic and committed group of architects and interior designers who love design and creative process. Our experience and constant research of trends allows us to meet expectations within the budget and translate a bold idea into non-standard design solutions. To get a truly functional and unsurpassed design of an office, apartment, restaurant, shopping and entertainment complex or a hotel, the right decision would be to contact the design studio Interio for help!

Our services

  • 01

    Consultation

    Help in choosing a style consistent with the interior, decorations, accessories, wall colors, help in planning the space and dividing it into coherent visual sections.

  • 02

    Complex project

    A holistic interior design project including detailed study of needs, expectations and preferences, inventory of the interior, inspiration boards, conceptual design, complete shopping list, 3D visualization.

  • 03

    Complex project + author's supervision

    After creating a comprehensive interior design, we monitor and control the finishing works until they are fully completed. We verify compliance of the works, answer contractors' questions, solve all problems constantly.

  • 04

    Turnkey project

    We take over all work on the project from you, including the provision of a reliable finishing team and the coordination of all orders and deliveries of equipment elements from the beginning till you receive keys.

BRISTOL 2022

On the two floors—each 1,485 square feet— she introduced furniture with rounded shapes and textiles for a warm feel while nods to Ukraine are reflected through objects made by craftsmen and artists.

To complement the wooden floor and niches, as well as the elements in Ceppo Lombardo stone, Molodykh painted the walls a soft white.

Note the double-height entrance hall, with Ceppo di gris limestone flooring, a custom mottled-brick accent wall, and a sculptural spiral staircase that corkscrews down to the lower level, partially embedded in the bedrock.

In the living room TV area: Edra On the Rocks sofa, Bolia coffee table, Gan Rugs carpet, Gubi floor lamp.

The bathroom is clad in Harmony Inspire Peronda tile, with an Uraldi mirror and Nova Luce light.

In the living room, a Servomuto wall lamp and Peter Upward painting hang above a Miniforms coffee table, and Kasthall rug; nearby, a Knoll chair pulls up to a desk with modular shelving by Made.

PRESTIGE HALL 2022

Located in the charming town of Campione d’Italia, this two-story apartment is as stunning inside as it is outside with its panoramic view of the mountains and Lake Lugano.

This airy Greenwich Village three-bedroom lives much larger than its 2,150 square feet would suggest, thanks to strategic sight lines, artfully integrated storage, and a careful attention to artificial and natural illumination.

The daughter’s room includes Muuto carpet, Kartell table, chair and side tables, Menu mirror, Atelier Areti lamp, and an &tradition small lamp.

The conjoined living area/study suits entertaining and overnighting alike: Timber doors slide closed to form cordoned-off guest quarters equipped with a bar, desk, and queen-size pullout.

Stone also features in more intimate spaces, like the primary bath, with a Cristallo marble–topped cerused-oak vanity and Perla Venata marble flooring.

TETRIS 2022

A lean-to structure became the dining room, where a Thonet chair services a custom blackened-oak table and a Jan Cools acrylic on canvas hangs on the wall.

Note the double-height entrance hall, with Ceppo di gris limestone flooring, a custom mottled-brick accent wall, and a sculptural spiral staircase that corkscrews down to the lower level, partially embedded in the bedrock.

In the front lounge, Jean-Marie Massoud’s Le Club armchair stands between custom lacquered built-ins, surrounding an existing but updated fireplace, and 1970’s Up & Up cocktail tables.

The living room bookshelf is custom-made by The Room Studio and features an armchair and auxiliary table by Roche Bobois. Photography by Mauricio Fuertes.

In the living room lounge area: Edra Cipria sofa by Fernando and Humberto Campana, Gubi Pacha chairs, Baxter coffee table, CC Tapis carpet, Ochre pendant.
